# Bioinformatics 

## The Problem
In recent years, the world of biology has been overflowing with massive quantities of data, far too large for humans to be able to comb through like they did in the past. This presented a good opportunity for an important overlap between the data management aspects of computer science and the massive amounts of data generated by things like genome sequencing.
